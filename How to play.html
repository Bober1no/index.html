üéØ Game Overview

üéÆ Objective

Block Shots is a unique fusion of Tetris and tower defense! Drop Tetris pieces to build defensive structures while waves of enemies march toward the top of your grid. Use different block types strategically to create powerful defenses and survive as long as possible.

üé≤ Core Mechanics

Tetris Gameplay: Standard Tetris controls - rotate, move, and drop pieces to fill your grid.

Tower Defense: Each block type has unique defensive properties that automatically activate against enemies.

Resource Management: Earn scraps by clearing lines and defeating enemies to purchase special abilities.

Progressive Difficulty: Enemies get stronger each level, but so do your rewards!

üèÜ Scoring

Earn points by clearing lines, defeating enemies, and surviving waves. Use the space bar for hard drops to get bonus points and scraps. Your score determines your level, which increases both difficulty and rewards!

Juggernauts: Only explosive mines (O-blocks) can damage them. Build mine fields to stop them!

Scaling: All enemies get stronger each level, but rewards scale too!

üí∞ Earning Scraps

Line Clears: Primary source of scraps. Clear multiple lines at once for bonus rewards!

Enemy Defeats: Each enemy type drops scraps when destroyed.

Hard Drops: Use spacebar for extra scraps with each piece placement.

Level Scaling: Higher levels give more scraps from all sources.

üéØ Strategy Tips

Helper Synergy: Place Helper blocks near your strongest defenses for maximum effect.

Resource Management: Save scraps for critical moments - don't spend them all immediately.

Mixed Defense: Combine different block types for layered defense strategies.

Emergency Options: Keep enough scraps for emergency mine purchases or grid shifts.
